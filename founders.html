Founders

God has used SIS.MARY VIJAY & PASTOR SAMUEL VIJAY as His instruments of grace in founding this ministry. Our precious saviour Jesus Christ equipped these servants of the living God with power from above in the year 1997 and right from then on they have been the vessels of the Lord in bringing healing to the soul, mind and body of incalculably many people.


The year 1997 was remarkable in  life of his servant Sis. Mary, as she came to know that  without Holy Spirit there was no spiritual growth. Then she began to kneel down and pray in the presence of the Lord for the anointing of the Holy Spirit.

One day suddenly the Lord blessed Sis. Mary with the Mighty anointing of the Holy spirit which was a like a great fire coming upon her. Also at the same time she was blessed with the precious gifts and she was able to prophesize with the anointing of the spirit. From then onwards the precious Saviour Lord Jesus began to speak to her and began to reveal his mysterious ways to her.

God revealed that they would move from Visakhapatnam to Hyderabad where their Ministry would start.

As the days passed by the Lord was speaking to her about His ministry. The precious saviour promised her a great healing and preaching ministry. Further, the Lord said that he was equipping Sis. Mary Vijay with a powerful preaching ministry so much that whenever she would stand to preach the Word of God, the mysteries of Scripture will be revealed through her to the congregation. The Lord further spoke to them and promised that whenever they preach it will be like a flow of fire coming upon the congregation.

This was the Prophesy of the Lord to Sis. Mary in the year 1996.

So then Sis. Mary started praying earnestly in the presence of the Lord and everyday the Lord was equipping Sis. Mary with his spiritual and his prophetic words.

Sis.Mary had started the  Prayer cell according the prophetic word of God in the year 1997.

In the very early stages of the Ministry a great tribulation came upon Sis.Mary and her Family. Her own Sister was affected with a rare disease with the Doctors proclaimed was incurable, that there was no chance for her to live.

On one side there was the prayer cell and God was bringing many people to the  weekly meetings, and was saving them and filling them with the Holy spirit and on one side her own sister was almost on her death bed.
But Sis.Mary did not lose heart but continued to pray unto the Utmost God of Gods and King of Kings who has all the authority in Heaven and this earth. Thus after continued fasting prayers Gods mighty wonder working Miracle happened in the Life of Sis.Mary’s Sister and our Good God healed her and even the Doctors were mystified and proclaimed that this was the work of God. All Glory to God!

The Lord gave his grace to his servants Sis.Mary and Pastor Samuel to start his Church in the 2003, Namely BETHANY HOUSE OF PRAYER - HYDERABAD - INDIA.

Today the Lord is leading and using his servants for his glory All glory is to our dear Lord.
